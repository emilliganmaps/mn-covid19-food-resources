Greater Minneapolis-Saint Paul food resources for kids during COVID-19


Food insecurity is an ongoing problem that is being exacerbated by school and business closures due to the COVID-19 outbreaks, and I created this to be a freely available resource to help communities in Minnesota during this crisis. I cannot guarantee the accuracy of this information, as this is a project I am taking on by myself in my spare time, and the information I gathered is either from public websites or input from others. I am a cartographer who loves public health mapping and the Midwest, and you can learn more about me and what I do here. A huge thanks to Chris Keller, who originally created the foundation of this code for the state of New Mexico and kindly allowed me to adapt it for MSP!
